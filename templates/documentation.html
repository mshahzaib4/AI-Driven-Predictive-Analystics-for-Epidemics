Introduction to HealthPredict Pro

HealthPredict Pro is an advanced AI-powered health prediction system designed to assess the risk of serious medical conditions. Our platform utilizes machine learning algorithms trained on extensive medical datasets to provide accurate risk assessments for lung cancer, heart disease, and liver disease.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Predictions: Advanced machine learning models with 90-98% accuracy
  • Early Detection: Identify potential health risks before symptoms manifest
  • Personalized Reports: Detailed risk assessments with probability percentages
  • Actionable Recommendations: Tailored health advice based on your results
  • Test Scenarios: Pre-filled examples to demonstrate system capabilities

The system is designed for both healthcare professionals and individuals who want to proactively monitor their health. All predictions should be followed up with professional medical consultation.

Getting Started

Using HealthPredict Pro is simple and intuitive. Follow these steps to assess your health risks:

  1. Navigate to the desired prediction module using the top menu (Lung Cancer, Heart Disease, or Liver Disease)
  2. Fill in the required health information in the form fields
  3. Use the test buttons to quickly fill the form with example data:
    • Fill Positive Test - Pre-fills with high-risk values
    • Fill Negative Test - Pre-fills with low-risk values
    • Run Auto Positive Test - Fills and submits high-risk example
    • Run Auto Negative Test - Fills and submits low-risk example
  4. Click "Predict" or "Analyze Risk" to generate your assessment
  5. Review your results including risk probability and recommendations

Important Note

HealthPredict Pro is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

Lung Cancer Prediction

The Lung Cancer Prediction module assesses your risk based on 15 key factors including smoking history, symptoms, and demographic information.

Required Information

  • Personal Information (Name, Age, Gender)
  • Smoking History (Current smoker, Years smoked)
  • Symptoms (Coughing, Shortness of breath, Chest pain, etc.)
  • Environmental Factors (Exposure to asbestos, radon, etc.)
  • Family History

Heart Disease Prediction

The Heart Disease Prediction module evaluates cardiovascular risk based on 19 health indicators including vital signs, lifestyle factors, and medical history.

Required Information

  • Personal Information (Name, Age, Gender)
  • Vital Signs (Blood pressure, Cholesterol levels)
  • Medical History (Diabetes, Family history of heart disease)
  • Lifestyle Factors (Exercise habits, Smoking, Alcohol consumption)
  • Biomarkers (CRP level, Homocysteine level, Triglycerides)

Prediction Model

Our heart disease prediction model uses a Gradient Boosting classifier trained on the Framingham Heart Study dataset with additional clinical parameters.

Liver Disease Prediction

The Liver Disease Prediction module analyzes liver health based on 10 key biomarkers from liver function tests and basic patient information.

Required Information

  • Personal Information (Name, Age, Gender)
  • Bilirubin Levels (Total and Direct)
  • Liver Enzymes (Alkaline Phosphatase, ALT, AST)
  • Protein Levels (Total Proteins, Albumin)
  • Albumin/Globulin Ratio

Prediction Model

Our liver disease prediction model uses a Support Vector Machine (SVM) classifier trained on the Indian Liver Patient Dataset with additional clinical parameters.

FAQ & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?

Our models achieve 94-98% accuracy on validation datasets. However, real-world accuracy may vary based on data quality and individual health factors.

Can I use this system for diagnosis?

No. HealthPredict Pro is a risk assessment tool, not a diagnostic system. All results should be reviewed by a qualified healthcare professional.

How often are the models updated?

We retrain our models quarterly with new data and continuously monitor performance to ensure accuracy.

Is my personal health data stored?

No personal health data is stored on our servers. All information entered is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after generating results.
